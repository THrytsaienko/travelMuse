The Best Books To Read For Travelling In Asia

Hi! I'm glad you are reading this! I'm a former investment banker from South California, who sold everything I owned back in September 2012 and bid my old life goodbye.

Goodbye in favor of travelling solo around the world searching for off the beaten path destinations in Asia and beyond and writing about it on this very travel blog.

Whether you're on a short holiday or a long-term trip, these tips will help you, photos will inspire you, and the stories will awaken your wanderlust.

I find lesser known places by speaking with locals, taking local transportation, and immersing myself in the culture. Then I show you how to do the same.
